Privacy Policy

📅 Last Updated: April 27, 2026  ·  Effective Date: April 27, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how GPS Land Area Measure ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, stores and protects your information when you use our Android application.

By downloading or using GPS Land Area Measure, you agree to the practices described in this policy. Please read it carefully. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

📋 1. Information We Collect

A. Account Information (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your Google account name
  • Your Google email address
  • Your Google User ID (a unique identifier)
  • Your profile picture (display purposes only)
ℹ️ Google Sign-In is completely optional. You can use the app fully in Guest Mode without providing any personal information.

B. Location Data

The app collects your precise GPS location to:

  • Record field boundaries, walking paths, and marker positions
  • Display your current position on the map
  • Calculate area, perimeter, and distance measurements

Location data is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit your location to our servers.

C. Measurement Data

All measurements (areas, lengths, locations), categories, names, descriptions, and notes you create are stored locally on your device using a private database. This data is not transmitted externally.

D. Photos

If you attach a photo to a measurement, the image is stored locally on your device. Photos are not uploaded to any server.

⚙️ 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to operate the app and deliver its features:

  • Display your measurements, categories, and saved locations in the app
  • Calculate area, perimeter, and distance using your GPS coordinates
  • Show your current position on the map
  • Associate your data with your account when signed in (Google User ID)
  • Enable sharing and export of your measurement data
✅ We use your data only to provide the service you asked for. Nothing else. Ever.

🚫 3. What We NEVER Do

We want to be crystal clear about what we do not do with your data:

✗ No data selling ✗ No data sharing ✗ No advertising ✗ No third-party tracking ✗ No analytics profiling ✗ No marketing emails ✗ No behaviour tracking

Your measurement data is private. We will never sell, rent, lease or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes.

🛡️ 5. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

All measurements, photos, and categories are stored locally on your device using Android's private app storage (Room/SQLite database). This data is accessible only by the GPS Land Area Measure app on your device.

Cloud Storage (Google Sign-In Only)

If you choose to sign in with Google, your account session is managed by Firebase Authentication — a secure, encrypted, enterprise-grade service operated by Google LLC.

  • All data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Data stored in Firebase is encrypted at rest
  • Firebase complies with ISO 27001, SOC 1, SOC 2, and SOC 3 standards
  • We do NOT store your Google password or authentication credentials

Guest Mode

In Guest Mode, all data stays exclusively on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

🔒 We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data. We recommend keeping your device secured with a PIN, pattern, or biometric lock.

🔗 6. Third-Party Services

GPS Land Area Measure integrates the following trusted third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Maps SDK — Displays map tiles and provides geocoding. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — Provides account authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Authentication — Manages user login sessions securely. Firebase Privacy Policy

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, crash reporters, or any other third-party tracking tools.

🗂️ 7. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the application. Uninstalling removes all local data permanently.
  • Account data (Google Sign-In): Firebase Authentication retains your sign-in session. You can sign out at any time from the navigation menu. If you request full account deletion, all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Guest data: Stored only on your device. Deleted when you uninstall the app or clear app data from phone settings.

✅ 8. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data at all times:

✓ Access your data ✓ Edit your data ✓ Delete your data ✓ Use Guest Mode ✓ Sign out anytime

How to Delete Individual Data

  • Delete a measurement: Open the measurement detail screen → tap the delete button.
  • Delete a category: Go to Categories → long press → Delete.
  • Delete all local data: Phone Settings → Apps → GPS Land Area Measure → Clear Data.

Account & Data Deletion Request

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. We will process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted:

  • Your Google Sign-In account link
  • All saved measurements (areas, lengths, locations)
  • All categories and groups
  • Photos attached to measurements
  • Any synced data stored in our database
