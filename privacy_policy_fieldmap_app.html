Privacy Policy

👋 Introduction

Welcome to GPS Land Area Measure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using GPS Land Area Measure, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the application and discontinue use.

📋 Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we receive your Google account name, email address, and profile photo from Google. This information is used solely to identify your account within the app.

2.2 Location Data

The app collects your precise GPS location to:

  • Record field boundaries, walking paths, and marker positions
  • Display your current position on the map
  • Calculate area, perimeter, and distance measurements

Location data is used only within the app and stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your location to our servers.

2.3 Photos

If you attach a photo to a measurement, the image is stored locally on your device. Photos are not uploaded to any server unless you explicitly use a sharing or export feature.

2.4 Measurement Data

All measurements (fields, distances, markers), groups, names, descriptions, and notes you create are stored locally on your device using a private database. This data is not transmitted externally.

🔐 App Permissions

Permission Why It Is Needed
Location (Fine & Coarse) To record GPS measurements, display your position on the map, and enable GPS mode recording.
Camera To take photos that can be attached to a measurement record.
Read Media / Storage To select existing photos from your gallery to attach to a measurement.
Internet To load Google Maps tiles, authenticate with Google Sign-In, and enable Firebase services.

🔗 Third-Party Services

GPS Land Area Measure integrates the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Maps SDK — Displays map tiles and provides geocoding. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — Provides authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Authentication — Manages user login sessions securely. Firebase Privacy Policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

🛡️ Data Storage & Security

All measurement data, photos, and groups are stored locally on your device using Android's private app storage. No measurement data is sent to external servers.

Account authentication is handled entirely by Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In — we do not store your Google password or authentication credentials on our servers.

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your device secure with a screen lock.

🗂️ Data Retention

Measurement data is retained on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the application. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data permanently.

Firebase Authentication retains your sign-in session data. You can sign out at any time from the app's navigation menu, which removes the local session.

👶 Children's Privacy

GPS Land Area Measure is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

✅ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — View all data stored by the app within the app itself.
  • Delete — Delete individual measurements, groups, or photos at any time.
  • Sign Out — Sign out of your Google account from the navigation menu at any time.
  • Uninstall — Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Last Updated date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
